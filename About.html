HomeValue+

In India, middle-class homeowners are constantly looking for ways to enhance the value of their properties. Our project, JFSD-PS22, provides a platform offering personalized recommendations and tools to make homes more attractive and valuable. The platform helps users improve the aesthetics and functionality of their properties, increasing long-term value and appeal. With curated suggestions for furniture, colors, wall art, and interior design, we aim to make home improvement more accessible to the Indian middle class.
